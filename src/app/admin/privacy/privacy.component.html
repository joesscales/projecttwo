Privacy Policy

We will always keep any data you provide to us safe and secure. This Privacy Policy summarises why we need your data and how we use it. By using our service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. It is possible view our site without providing any personal details but you won't be able to purchase caps without sharing your details.

Information we collect

We may collect the following data about you. We'll only collect information where it is knowingly provided by you. For example, when you:

  • Sign up to our mailing list
  • Purchase a product from our website
  • Email us directly

The type of personal information we may collect includes:

  • Name
  • Delivery Address

What we do with the information we collect

We use the personal information to provide for:

  • Fulfilling orders - we need to complete orders you make on the site, send you email confirmations and deliver your caps
  • Customer service purposes - so we can reply to any queries you send through
  • Internal record keeping - so you can view previous orders made
  • Send promotional emails about new products or special offers which we think may be of interest to you

Who we share your information with

  • We use SendGrid to store email sign up information and to send out our marketing emails.
  • We use Royal Mail to deliver your cap to your door so therefore need to share your details with them
  • As a payment provider, we use Stripe so your information will therefore also be shared with Stripe when completing an order with us. Please note that any card details are held securely Stripe and cannot be accessed by us.

Cookies

Cookies are not used to identify individuals - only demographic groups. We use cookies when you visit our site to give you a better experience and to analyse our website traffic. You can choose to not allow some cookies but this may impact your experience on the site.

What are cookies?

Cookies are data files which can hold small amounts of info and they are stored on your device (computer, smartphone, etc) when you first visit a website.

How we use cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Site navigation
  • Allowing you to add items to your basket
  • Analysing users and their behaviours on the site
